About

I am a PhD student at the Faculty of Social Studies, Masaryk University, supervised by Prof. XY. My research examines how partnership status shapes intergenerational relationships and family support systems across European welfare regimes.

My dissertation is part of the PRINS project funded by GAČR. I study partnership trajectories as mechanisms of social stratification, with a focus on how different types of singles access family support. My work bridges sociology and economics, examining how family support systems respond to visible life transitions but overlook chronic states like lifelong singlehood.

Research interests: intergenerational inequality, family sociology, welfare state regimes, life course research, causal inference
